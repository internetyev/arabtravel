Hotel Eden Rock Hotel Namaa Bay

Tripadvisor rating

One of our bestsellers in Sharm El Sheikh! This property is 3 minutes walk from the beach. Featuring stunning views of the Red Sea and the Sinai desert mountains, this hotel in Naama Bay offers rooms with a private balcony or terrace. Facilities include an outdoor pool.

The air-conditioned rooms of Eden Rock Hotel are decorated with wooden furniture and large windows. All rooms have satellite TV, a minibar, and an en suite bathroom. Some rooms have a seating area.

Guests can enjoy local and international cuisine in main restaurant Fever, which overlooks the pool. Pool bar Parrot offers a wide variety of drinks and panoramic views. Indoor bar Wave is open 24/7.

A staircase leads from Eden Rock?s pool area to a private, sandy beach area and Naama Bay?s marina. The hotel also offers massage services, laundry facilities, and a 24-hour reception.

Several shops, restaurants and bars are within walking distance of Eden Rock Hotel. Sharm el-Sheikh International Airport is just 15 km from the hotel.
